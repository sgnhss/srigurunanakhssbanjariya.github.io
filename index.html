Welcome to Guru Nanak HSS Banjariya

Guru Nanak HSS Banjariya, one of the most sought after schools in Banjariya, Saidnagar, Rampur Dt Uttar Pradesh, offers a blend of Primary, Upper Primary and Higher Secondary School Programs. Established in the year 2011, the school welcomes new students every year. Taught by qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty, the school curriculum is centered on providing the best possible learning opportunities to students. The school is affiliated to Uttar Pradesh Madhymic Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP).

Our mission is to help students identify and achieve meaningful goals that will help them develop strong academic and life skills.
